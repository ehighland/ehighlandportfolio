Art

I have always enjoyed art as a hobby, but particularly so as a kid. I drew all the time. It is harder to keep up with hobbies as an adult, but I try to draw every now and again. I typically draw in ink on paper, scan or take a photo of that, and color/refine in GIMP. I also use GIMP for photo editing or other design projects.
